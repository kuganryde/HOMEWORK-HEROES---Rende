# Homework Hero - Project Documentation

## 1. Project Overview
**Homework Hero** is a cutting-edge synchronization platform designed specifically for **Sekolah Jenis Kebangsaan (Tamil) Bandar Mahkota Cheras**. Our goal is to bridge the communication gap between the classroom and the home using AI-powered tools and real-time data tracking.

## 2. Technical Stack (Architect-7 Selection)
We have selected a "Modern Performance" stack to ensure the app is fast, scalable, and intelligent:
- **Frontend**: React 19 + TypeScript (for type-safe, robust code).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (for a modern, responsive mobile-first UI).
- **AI Core**: Google Gemini 3 (Pro & Flash) for homework analysis and hints.
- **State/Storage**: Simulated Firebase/Cloud Architecture (using LocalStorage for this prototype).
- **Icons**: Lucide React for intuitive visual navigation.

## 3. Core Features & Functions

### Cultural & Community Integration (New)
- **Malaysian Festival Themes**: The app automatically observes major Malaysian holidays (CNY, Hari Raya, Deepavali, etc.), updating the dashboard theme and displaying animated cultural graphics.
- **Birthday Celebrations**: Students receive a special animated pop-up with confetti and a personalized message on their birthday to foster a positive school culture.

### For Teachers (The Administrators)
- **Bulk Assignment Flow**: Assign homework to the entire class or specific students in seconds.
- **Academic Momentum Charts**: Visual trend analysis showing completion rates for individual students.
- **Profile Verification**: A secure gateway where teachers must approve student name or class changes requested by parents.
- **Live Progress Tracker**: Real-time view of who has acknowledged and completed tasks.
- **Data Consolidation**: All feeds (students, homework, approvals) are paginated (8 items per page) for maximum scannability and performance.

### For Parents (The Partners)
- **Digital Acknowledgement**: One-tap "Receipt" confirmation to let teachers know the parent is aware of the task.
- **AI Homework Hints**: Gemini-powered simplified explanations to help parents guide their children without providing direct answers.
- **Image Analysis**: Parents can snap a photo of a worksheet, and Gemini will analyze and summarize the requirements.
- **Student Management**: Request profile updates that remain pending until the school verifies them.

### For Students (The Learners)
- **Gamified XP System**: Students earn Experience Points (XP) for completing homework based on task priority.
- **10-Rank Progression**: A journey from "Seedling" to "Universe" with unique icons and titles to motivate consistent effort.
- **Clear Task Visibility**: Organized view of priority-ranked tasks with pagination for easy navigation.
- **AI Assistant**: A friendly bot to answer school-related questions and provide study tips.
- **Heritage Awareness**: A section dedicated to the school's identity and student-led development.

## 4. Feature Capabilities
- **Real-time Synchronization**: Instant updates across teacher and parent dashboards using a simulated cloud architecture.
- **AI-Powered Intelligence**: Leverages Google Gemini 3 for complex image analysis, homework summarization, and parent-focused study hints.
- **Gamification Engine**: Automated XP calculation and rank progression logic based on task completion and priority.
- **Dynamic Theme Engine**: Context-aware UI that automatically adapts its visual style based on the Malaysian cultural calendar.
- **Automated Event Triggers**: System-level detection of student birthdays and school milestones with celebratory UI components.
- **Responsive Data Pagination**: High-performance data handling that ensures smooth navigation even with large student rosters and task feeds.
- **Secure Approval Workflow**: Multi-step verification process for sensitive student data changes, ensuring school record integrity.

## 5. Benefits to the School
1. **Transparency**: Eliminates "I forgot my homework" excuses through parent-teacher synchronization.
2. **Gamification**: Boosts student engagement and motivation through the XP and Ranking system.
3. **Cultural Inclusivity**: Celebrates the diverse Malaysian heritage of the SJK(T) community through dynamic themes.
4. **Data-Driven Insights**: Teachers can identify struggling students early through the "Momentum Chart."
5. **AI Integration**: Positions Homework Hero as a forward-thinking digital leader in the district.
6. **Security**: Centralized control over student data with teacher-only final approval rights.
